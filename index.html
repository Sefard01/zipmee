How to Use ZipMee

Just 3 simple steps to start analyzing your WhatsApp chat.

Step 1

Export Your Chat from WhatsApp

Open any WhatsApp chat. Tap the 3-dot menu (top right), then go to More → Export Chat. With or without media — both formats work perfectly.

On Android you'll get a .zip file. On iPhone you'll get a .txt file. ZipMee supports both.

Step 2

Upload the File to ZipMee

Open the app. Click the upload button or simply drag & drop your file. Processing starts immediately — even with 50,000+ messages.

No login required. No account needed. Just select your file and go.

Step 3

Explore Your Analytics

Your chat loads in WhatsApp style instantly. Click the Analytics button to open the sidebar — find word stats, response times, message counts, and more.

Date filters and message search are also available in the header.
